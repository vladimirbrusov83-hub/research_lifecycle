The honest part

Where the lanes overlap — suggestion, or record?

Agentic AI reaches onto library turf in four phases. The split isn't clean — so ask a sharper question: is the output a suggestion, or a record that has to stand up in review?

Phase 02 · Literature Review

Both claim to "find everything, rigorously." The agent retrieves; the library's PRISMA protocol is the auditable method of record.

The floor an agent can't reach

Four things only the library can do

Not a services list — the structural capabilities no model has, by construction. This is the budget case and the reassurance in one.

01

Licensed access

The corpus behind the paywall

An agent can only reason over what it can reach. Subscribed databases and archival holdings are reachable because the institution licenses them.

02

Permanence

A DOI that resolves in 30 years

Preservation is a commitment across time, not an output. Repository deposit and archival access outlive any model or funding cycle.

03

Authority

Determinations that must stand

A predatory-journal call or integrity finding has to hold up in tenure, funding and misconduct review. An agent's opinion has no standing there.

04

Neutrality

No optimization target

Agentic tools are tuned toward an objective — acceptance, engagement, novelty. The library isn't optimizing for anything but the researcher's aims.
